Delete your MoodField account and data

MoodField ("we"), operated by Wiktor Tomasik (sole proprietor, ul. Poznańska 10, Kraków, Poland), gives you two ways to delete every piece of personal data we hold about you, in line with GDPR Article 17 and Google Play's account-deletion policy.

Option 1 — From inside the app (recommended, takes 10 seconds)

  1. Open MoodField.
  2. Tap the Account tab at the top of the screen.
  3. Open Settings → Withdraw consent & delete my data.
  4. Confirm. The app wipes the device, asks the server to cascade-delete the cloud copy, and signs you out.

The deletion is irreversible. Subscription cancellation is handled separately by Google Play — visit play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions to stop billing.

Option 2 — Email request

Email pixedar@gmail.com from the address on your account. Include:

  • The email address you sign in with
  • The approximate date you created the account

We confirm identity within 5 business days, then complete deletion within 30 days (GDPR limit).

What gets deleted

  • Every mood / dream / journal entry you created
  • Every embedding derived from your entries
  • Your render exports stored in cloud storage
  • Account profile (display name, email)
  • The Firebase Auth account itself
  • Crash logs older than 90 days are removed automatically

What does NOT get deleted

  • Anonymised aggregate statistics that cannot be re-identified to you.
  • Records the law requires us to keep (e.g. tax invoices for paid subscriptions, retained for 6 years per Polish accounting law).

Questions

Right to lodge a complaint

You can lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority. In Poland: Prezes Urzędu Ochrony Danych Osobowych (UODO), ul. Stawki 2, 00-193 Warszawa, uodo.gov.pl.
